Our products

Our range includes new and used pallets — EPAL / EUR pallets, CP1–CP9 chemical pallets, H1 hygienic plastic pallets, industrial pallets, one-way pallets and other solutions tailored to your needs.

Wooden pallets

EPAL / EUR pallets

Certified Euro pallets 1200 × 800 mm — the backbone of European logistics. Available new and used, suitable for exchange systems and compliant with European standards.

Chemical industry

Chemical pallets CP1 – CP9

Standardised CP1 to CP9 pallets developed for the chemical industry. Various dimensions and load capacities depending on your operation and the material transported.

Plastic pallets

H1 hygienic plastic pallets

Hygienic plastic pallets ideal for the food, pharmaceutical and healthcare industries. Easy to wash, durable and suitable for operations with strict hygiene requirements.

Industrial

Industrial pallets

Pallets for industrial use in various dimensions and designs — matched to your production, warehousing or distribution requirements.

Export

One-way pallets

An economical solution for one-way transport and export. Made to customer dimensions with no return logistics required.
