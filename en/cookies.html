Cookie Policy

Effective from: 16 July 2026  ·  Tom Kave Company s. r. o., trading as TS Palletium

1. Introduction

This Cookie Policy explains how Tom Kave Company s. r. o., trading as TS Palletium, uses cookies and similar technologies on the website www.tspalletium.com.

When using our website, you may be asked to consent to the use of certain categories of cookies via the cookie banner.

2. What cookies are

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help the website function properly, improve user experience and allow us to collect statistical information about site traffic.

3. What cookies we use

Essential cookies

These cookies are required for the website to function properly. Without them, basic features such as submitting the contact form or saving your cookie preferences cannot be provided. These cookies cannot be switched off.

Analytics cookies

With the visitor's consent, we may use analytics tools to measure website traffic. This data helps us understand:

  • how many visitors came to the website,
  • which pages are visited most,
  • how visitors move around the site,
  • how we can improve the website.

Marketing cookies

The TS Palletium website currently does not use marketing cookies. If advertising or remarketing tools are implemented in the future, they will only be used with your consent.

4. Third-party cookies

Our website may in the future use third-party services, for example:

  • Google Analytics,
  • Google Tag Manager,
  • Google Maps,
  • YouTube,
  • reCAPTCHA.

The use of these services may result in third-party cookies being stored in accordance with their own privacy policies.

5. How you can manage cookies

On your first visit to the website, the cookie banner allows you to:

  • accept all cookies,
  • reject optional cookies,
  • adjust your preferences.

You can change or withdraw your consent at any later time. You can also delete or block cookies in your web browser. Please note that blocking essential cookies may affect the proper functioning of the website.

6. Changes to this Cookie Policy

Tom Kave Company s. r. o. reserves the right to update this Cookie Policy as appropriate in the event of changes to legislation or to the technical implementation of the website. The current version of this document is always available at www.tspalletium.com.
