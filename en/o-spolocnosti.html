About TS Palletium

Connecting pallet manufacturers, suppliers and customers into a reliable trading network for all of Europe.

Who we are

TS Palletium is a modern B2B company focused on the purchase, sale, supply and logistics coordination of wooden and plastic pallets across the European market.

We connect manufacturers, suppliers and customers into a reliable trading network that ensures stable pallet supply for the manufacturing, logistics and distribution sectors. Our goal is to bring the stability, efficiency and transparency that modern supply chains demand.

Our purpose is to help manufacturers, suppliers and customers trade pallets more simply, faster, more safely and at better value.

What we do

01

Pallet buyback & purchasing

We buy new and used EUR, industrial and other types of wooden pallets from companies across Europe. The entire process is fast, transparent and logistically coordinated.

02

Pallet supply

We supply new and used wooden and plastic pallets for production, warehousing, logistics and distribution — to individual customer requirements and in line with European standards.

03

Logistics coordination

We coordinate the movement of pallets between suppliers and customers to ensure reliable deliveries, efficient use of capacity and smooth logistics processes.

Our approach

In modern logistics, a pallet is not just a carrier. It is an integral part of supply chain infrastructure.

That is why we focus on creating a stable, predictable and efficient flow of pallets that keeps production, warehousing and distribution running smoothly.

Every business and logistics process we build stands on four pillars:

  • reliability
  • transparency
  • flexibility
  • long-term partnership

Our vision

Our ambition is to become one of the most reliable partners for trading wooden and plastic pallets in Europe.

We believe the future of the pallet industry lies in greater connectivity, efficient logistics and transparent cooperation between manufacturers, suppliers and customers. That is why we are gradually building a strong European trading network to make today's fragmented market work more efficiently.

The long-term vision of TS Palletium is to build a leading European trading platform that connects pallet manufacturers, suppliers and customers and makes trading across Europe simpler.

Partnerships

We work with manufacturing companies, logistics operators, distribution centres, warehousing companies and industrial firms across Europe.

Wherever pallets are an integral part of daily operations, we bring stability, reliability, efficient coordination and long-term business partnership.
